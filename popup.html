📖 How It Works
Core features
⚡
Autocomplete as you type On any supported AI site, start typing and matching saved prompts appear in a dropdown. Click to insert. Arrow keys to navigate.
💾
Save button near input A floating Save button appears near the AI input box. Click it to save what you just typed. Drag the ⠿ handle to move it anywhere.
⌨️
Ctrl+Space to browse all Press Ctrl+Space on any AI site to open a dropdown of all your saved prompts — even without typing anything first.
🏷️
Tag filtering Type ;email to instantly filter to prompts tagged "email". Type ;code for code prompts. Works in the autocomplete dropdown.
Organisation
📌
Pin top prompts Click ⭐ on any prompt to pin it. Pinned prompts always appear first in suggestions.
📦
Starter packs Go to the 📦 Packs tab. Load the Founder Toolkit, Developer Pack, Content Writing, or Marketing Pack — 5 ready prompts each.
📜
Chat History Navigator A small button appears next to the Save button on AI chat sites. Click it to see all your questions in the current chat — click any to jump to it.
